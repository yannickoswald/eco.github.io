Quantitative Ecological Economics

Integrating biophysical scale with economic analysis

Welcome

This open-access book introduces the quantitative tools needed to study how material and energy flows shape — and are shaped by — the monetary economy. We combine ideas from ecological economics, physics and data science, moving from back-of-the-envelope scaling to fully fledged empirical models.

The project is intentionally lean: each chapter is a self-contained lesson you can read in a browser or launch as an interactive notebook. Feel free to fork the repository and translate the text. At all times cite this book source as

Oswald, Y. (2025). Quantitative Ecological Economics.
